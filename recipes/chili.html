Instant Pot Chili

A hearty beef chili that can be conveniently cooked in an Instant Pot pressure cooker.

Special Equipment

  • 6-quart Instant Pot

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sunflower oil
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground chuck
  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • One 15-ounce can fire-roasted tomatoes
  • One 15-ounce can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Set Instant Pot to normal saute setting and add oil.
  2. When oil is hot, add ground chuck, chili powder and kosher salt. Cook and break apart meat until browned, for about five (5) minutes.
  3. Add diced onion and minced garlic. Stir and cook for one (1) minute.
  4. Add tomatoes, pinto beans, Worcestershire sauce, and cocoa. Stir to combine.
  5. Cover with the Instant Pot pressure cooker lid and set to lock position.
  6. Set to pressure cook on high for ten (10) minutes and start.
  7. After pressure-cook cycle is complete, perform a quick release and wait until complete.
  8. Stir and season with additional salt if desired. Serve over rice and/or topped with shredded cheddar, sour cream, or pickled jalapeños.
